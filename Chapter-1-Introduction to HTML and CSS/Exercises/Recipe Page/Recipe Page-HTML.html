Grilled Chicken

This Middle Eastern grilled chicken is so juicy and delicious. It goes perfectly on top of a salad, or with some rice, and even in a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 350 gm chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder

Method

  1. Wash the chicken
  2. Them with a fork.
  3. In a large bowl, combine hung curd, ginger paste, garlic paste, olive oil, red chilli powder,
    coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper, salt and garam masala. Stir all the ingredients
    together until well blended.
  4. add the chicken pieces and apply the masala mixture all over them.
  5. and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or up to 24 hours.
  6. Once done, preheat the oven at 190 degree Celsius.
  7. Remove the chicken pieces from the marinade and place them on a grilling tray.
  8. Grill them for 1 hour, flipping them after 30 minutes so that both sides can cook evenly.
  9. Check if the chicken has fully cooked by taking one piece out and cutting it with a knife.
  10. Serve hot with salad or a dip.
